For my Ishu

✦

You are not just the girl I love —
you are all nine forms of Durga Maa, living and breathing in my life.

A love letter

"They say Durga Maa has nine forms — each one a different kind of strength, grace, and love. I don't have to look very far to find all nine. I just have to look at you, Ishu."

On this Maha Ashtami, I want you to know how deeply I see you — not just as the person who holds my heart, but as someone who carries the divine in the most quiet, beautiful ways. Every form of the goddess I have found in you.

🌺

Nav Durga

The Nine Forms of Ishu

```
Form I
🌙
Shailputri
Daughter of the Mountains

Shailputri is the daughter of the Himalayas — unshakeable, grounded, born from the earth itself. She is the foundation upon which everything else rests.

You are my ground, Ishu. When the world spins too fast, you are the stillness I come back to. My mountain.

Form II
🌿
Brahmacharini
The Devoted One

Brahmacharini embodies devotion, patience, and discipline. She walks the path of wisdom with quiet fire burning inside her, never wavering.

In the way you commit to everything you love — fully, fiercely, without half measures — I see her in you every single day.

Form III
🔔
Chandraghanta
The Bell of the Moon

Chandraghanta is courage and beauty woven together — she wears a crescent moon and her roar silences fear. Grace and bravery, inseparable.

The way you face hard things with such elegance — scared but still standing — that is you, my Chandraghanta.

Form IV
🌌
Kushmanda
The Creator of the Universe

Kushmanda created the universe with a single smile. She is light, warmth, and the cosmic energy that gives life to all things around her.

When you smile, Ishu — the whole room changes. You don't just brighten things, you create warmth where there was none.

Form V
🌸
Skandamata
Mother of Courage

Skandamata is the goddess who nurtures the warrior. Fierce in her love, she protects those she holds dear with a ferocity that moves mountains.

The way you love the people in your life — with that protective, unconditional fierceness — is one of the most goddess-like things about you.

Form VI
🏔️
Katyayani
The Warrior Goddess

Katyayani is the goddess of righteous anger — born to fight what is wrong, to stand for truth even when it costs something.

You never let wrong things slide. You speak up when it matters. That quiet fire in you — that is Katyayani, and I admire it deeply.

Form VII
🌑
Kaalratri
The Dark Night

Kaalratri is the destroyer of fear and ignorance. She walks through darkness not because she is not afraid, but because the light she carries is enough.

You have walked through your own dark nights and come out with that light still in your eyes. That is the most extraordinary thing I know about you.

Form VIII
✨
Mahagauri
The Radiant One

Mahagauri is pure, radiant, and deeply peaceful. She is the goddess of serenity — the one who has passed through every storm and emerged luminous.

There is a softness about you, Ishu, that is not weakness — it is power at peace. That is Mahagauri. That is you.

Form IX
🪷
Siddhidatri
Giver of Blessings

Siddhidatri is the final, complete form — the goddess who grants wishes, bestows wisdom, and makes the impossible possible. She is wholeness itself.

You are my answered prayer, Ishu. Every good thing in my life got better the moment you came into it. You are my Siddhidatri.

```
🪔

On this Ashtami

May Maa Durga Bless You

  • With the strength to face every challenge with grace
  • With peace that no chaos can disturb
  • With joy that fills every room you walk into
  • With love that grows deeper with every passing year
  • With the courage to always be fully, beautifully yourself
  • With all the happiness you so effortlessly give to others
🌺

Ishu,

I wrote this because no gift felt big enough. And then I realised — nothing ever will, because what you are to me is bigger than any gift.

You are my goddess in the most real, everyday, beautiful sense of the word. Not distant or untouchable — but right here, warm and laughing and mine.

Maa Durga has nine forms. And I find every single one of them in you.

Shubh Maha Ashtami, my Ishu. 🌸

— Always yours 🪔
🪔

Jai Maa Durga
